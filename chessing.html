CHESS

“ Chess is war over the board. The object is to crush the opponent's mind. ”

- Bobby Fischer (Late American Chess Prodigy, Grandmaster)

My interest in chess was initially boosted with an objective of defeating my dad in chess. However, as I played more and more, I was able to visualise chess in a different manner. I could see myself as the King, making clever moves to check-mate my opponent. Morever, winning in chess involves lot of deep analysis and logic. Though the inception of chess engines may pose a threat to how humans play chess, I strictly believe that Man will always prevail in a 'Man vs Machine' scenario. These are some of my favourite players :

  • Magnus Carlsen
  • Bobby Fischer
  • The rivals - Karpov and Kasparov
  • Vishwanathan Anand
